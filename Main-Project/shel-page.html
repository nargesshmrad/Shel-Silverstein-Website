Welcome to my world

I am Sheldon Allan Silverstein. An American writer, poet, cartoonist, singer-songwriter, musician, and playwright. I was Born and raised in Chicago.As a children's author, some of my most acclaimed works include The Giving Tree, Where the Sidewalk Ends, and A Light in the Attic. As a songwriter, I wrote the 1969 Johnny Cash track "A Boy Named Sue", which peaked at number 2 on the U.S. Billboard Hot 100. My songs have been recorded and popularized by a wide range of other acts including Tompall Glaser, The Irish Rovers and Dr. Hook & the Medicine Show. I was the recipient of two Grammy Awards as well as nominations at the Golden Globe Awards and Academy Awards.
